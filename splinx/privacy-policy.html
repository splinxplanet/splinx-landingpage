At Splinx Planet, we prioritize the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard it. By using Splinx Planet and its related services (collectively referred to as "Services"), you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide, improve, and personalize our Services. This information includes:

a) Personal Information

Personal Information refers to any data that can be used to identify you as an individual. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, password, and other details necessary for account creation and management.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information to personalize your profile, such as a profile picture or social media handles.
  • Payment Information: When you make payments through our app, we collect information related to your payment method, such as credit card details or bank account information. We do not store full payment card information; it is processed by third-party payment processors.

b) Usage Data

We collect data on how you interact with our Services, which may include:

  • Log Data: Information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type. We also collect data on the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.
  • App Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our app, such as features used, content viewed, and settings configured. This helps us understand how users engage with our Services and where improvements are needed.

c) Location Data

With your consent, we may collect and process information about your location. This data may be used to provide location-based services, customize the app experience based on your location, and for other purposes such as analytics.

d) Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Services and store certain information. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that allow us to recognize your preferences and personalize your experience.

Types of Cookies Used:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device for a specified period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences and settings for future visits.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These are set by external services to collect data for analytics, advertising, and other purposes.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

a) Providing and Maintaining the Services

Your information is essential for us to operate our Services effectively. This includes creating and managing your account, processing transactions, and delivering customer support.

We use data to understand how you use our Services, identify trends, and make improvements. Personalization features, such as tailored content and recommendations, are based on your usage patterns and preferences.

c) Communicating with You

d) Processing Transactions

Payment information is used to process and complete transactions you make within the app. We use secure, third-party payment processors to handle payment information.

e) Ensuring Security and Compliance

We use your information to protect the integrity of our Services, detect and prevent fraud, and ensure compliance with legal obligations. This includes monitoring activity on our platform to identify potential security risks.

f) Legal and Regulatory Requirements

We may process your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes. This may include responding to requests from law enforcement or government authorities.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:

a) With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties if you have given us explicit permission to do so. For example, you may choose to share your activities on Splinx Planet with social media platforms.

We work with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our Services. These providers may perform tasks such as payment processing, data hosting, analytics, and customer support. They are bound by contractual obligations to protect your information and use it only for the purposes of providing services to us.

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations, such as responding to court orders or subpoenas.
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety, as well as those of our users or the public.

d) Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

e) Aggregated or Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated or anonymized data that cannot be used to identify you. This data may be used for analytics, research, and marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use encryption to protect your data during transmission and storage.
  • Access Controls: Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job functions.
